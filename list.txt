! Shorts in search results(keeps shorts in history)
www.youtube.com##:matches-path(/^(?!\/feed\/history).*$/)grid-shelf-view-model
www.youtube.com##:matches-path(/^(?!\/feed\/history).*$/)ytd-video-renderer:has([aria-label="Shorts"])
!Shorts search button
www.youtube.com##yt-chip-cloud-chip-renderer:nth-of-type(2):has-text(Shorts)
